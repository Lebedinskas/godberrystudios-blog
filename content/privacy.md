## 1. Data Controller

Godberry Studios is a sole proprietorship operated from the Republic of Lithuania and is the data controller for personal data processed via [godberrystudios.com](https://godberrystudios.com).

**Contact for privacy matters:** hello@godberrystudios.com

## 2. What We Collect and Why

We deliberately collect as little personal data as possible.

**a) Cloudflare Web Analytics (aggregate statistics).** We use Cloudflare's privacy-first analytics to understand which articles are read and how visitors reach the site. Cloudflare Web Analytics does **not** use cookies, does **not** assign persistent identifiers, and does **not** build individual visitor profiles. We receive only aggregate, non-identifying statistics (e.g. total page views per article, referring domains, country-level geography).

- **Legal basis:** Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) — measuring traffic to improve the site. We have assessed that this processing has minimal privacy impact because no cookies or persistent identifiers are involved.

**b) Newsletter subscription (if you opt in).** If you voluntarily provide your email address to subscribe to our newsletter, we store that email address and send you periodic updates about new articles and tools.

- **Legal basis:** Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR). You can withdraw consent at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link in any email or by contacting us.

We do **not** collect names, phone numbers, payment information, precise location data, device fingerprints, or behavioural profiles on this website.

## 3. Cookies

This website does **not** set any cookies. No consent banner is shown because none is required under the ePrivacy Directive for a cookieless site.

## 5. How Long We Keep Your Data

- **Analytics data:** Cloudflare Web Analytics retains aggregate data for up to 6 months; we ourselves do not store any analytics data beyond what is visible in the Cloudflare dashboard.
- **Newsletter email:** We keep your email until you unsubscribe or ask us to delete it. On unsubscribe, your email is removed within 30 days.

## 6. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are in the European Economic Area, the UK, or Switzerland, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal data we hold about you
- **Rectify** inaccurate data
- **Erase** your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Restrict** or **object to** processing
- **Data portability** — receive your data in a machine-readable format
- **Withdraw consent** at any time (for newsletter subscriptions)
- **Lodge a complaint** with your national data protection authority. In Lithuania this is the State Data Protection Inspectorate (Valstybinė duomenų apsaugos inspekcija — VDAI): <https://vdai.lrv.lt>.

To exercise any of these rights, email **hello@godberrystudios.com**. We will respond within 30 days as required by Art. 12(3) GDPR.

## 7. Security

We rely on Cloudflare's infrastructure security (TLS encryption in transit, DDoS protection, WAF). Our newsletter list (if any) is stored with industry-standard access controls. We will notify affected individuals and the supervisory authority within 72 hours if a personal data breach likely results in a risk to your rights (Art. 33–34 GDPR).

## 8. Children

This website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it.

## 9. International Data Transfers

Some of our service providers (notably Cloudflare) are based in the United States. Where data is transferred outside the EEA, we rely on the Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the European Commission and, where applicable, the EU–US Data Privacy Framework.

## 10. Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy if our practices change. Material changes will be announced on this page with a new effective date. Continued use of the website after a change means you accept the updated policy.
