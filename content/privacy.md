## Who We Are

Godberry Studios ("we", "us", "our") operates godberrystudios.com. This policy explains how we collect and use information when you visit our website.

## What We Collect

**Analytics Data:** We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, which is a privacy-first analytics tool. It does not use cookies, does not track individual users, and does not collect personal data. We only see aggregate statistics like total page views and top pages.

**Newsletter Subscription:** If you voluntarily subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address. We use this solely to send you updates about new articles and tools. You can unsubscribe at any time.

**No Cookies:** This website does not set any cookies or use any tracking scripts beyond Cloudflare Analytics.

## How We Use Your Data

- Analytics data is used to understand which content is helpful and improve the website
- Email addresses are used exclusively for sending newsletter updates
- We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to third parties

## Third-Party Services

Our tools are hosted on the [Apify platform](https://apify.com). When you use our tools through Apify, their privacy policy applies to that interaction.

## Your Rights

You may request deletion of your email address from our newsletter at any time by unsubscribing or contacting us.
